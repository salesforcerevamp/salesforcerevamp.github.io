Security & Trust

At Salesforce Revamp, security is at the core of everything we build. We design features that enhance Salesforce productivity while maintaining the highest standards of privacy, reliability, and transparency. Here’s how we safeguard your trust.

Infrastructure & Architecture

Local Browser Extension

Salesforce Revamp runs entirely inside your browser. It does not transmit Salesforce data to any external server — all processing happens locally on your machine.

Secure Update Mechanism

All updates are distributed via the verified Chrome Web Store pipeline, ensuring integrity and automatic patch delivery without manual downloads.

Minimal Access & Permissions

Only the permissions necessary for Salesforce UI enhancements are requested. The extension never modifies or transmits your data unless you explicitly choose to.

Data Privacy & User Trust

No Data Collected

Salesforce Revamp does not collect personal information or analytics. Your Salesforce metadata remains private and confined within your session.

OAuth & Session Handling

The extension relies on your active Salesforce session. Credentials or tokens are never stored, handled, or shared by Salesforce Revamp.

Compliance & Best Practices

We maintain a lean, audited codebase and follow security best practices, including dependency reviews and HTTPS enforcement for all external resources.

Your Role & Best Practices

Keep Your Browser Up-to-Date

Always use the latest version of Chrome or compatible browsers to ensure you benefit from the newest security improvements.

Install Only From Official Store

Download Salesforce Revamp exclusively from the Chrome Web Store. Avoid installing unofficial or modified versions.

Monitor Permissions

Review extension permissions in your browser settings. Salesforce Revamp lists every scope it uses transparently in its store listing.
